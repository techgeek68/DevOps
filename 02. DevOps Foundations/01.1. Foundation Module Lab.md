### Lab F.B: Waterfall Failure and Agile Success Research

**Objective**: Understand why model choice matters using real outcomes.

**What you need**
- Internet access
- Text editor
- 60 to 90 minutes

**Prerequisite**: Read F.2.

**Instructions**
1. Choose one Waterfall failure case and one Agile success case
2. Write 400 to 500 words with this structure:
   - Waterfall case summary
   - Root cause linked to model limitations
   - Agile case summary
   - Contrast and what Agile changed
   - Conditions where Waterfall is still appropriate
3. Include at least two sources per case

**Suggested source types**
- Official audit reports and government publications
- Organization engineering blogs and incident writeups
- IEEE and ACM case studies
- Martin Fowler style practitioner analysis

**Expected output**: Structured comparison with source list

### Lab F.C: DevOps Infinity Loop Diagram

**Objective**: Internalize DevOps lifecycle stages and tools by building a reference diagram.

**What you need**: Paper or diagram tool like draw.io or Mermaid

**Prerequisite**: Read F.3.

**Instructions**
1. Draw a full loop with stages:
   Plan, Code, Build, Test, Release, Deploy, Operate, Monitor
2. Add at least two tools per stage
3. Add flow arrows, including Monitor back to Plan
4. Add one sentence annotation per stage

| Stage | Tools to Label |
